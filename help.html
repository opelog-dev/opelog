Opelog Help & Support

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Opelog is a simple opened-date log app that lets you record when you opened cosmetics, skincare products, household items, and more, together with photos.

Opelog helps you remember “When did I open this?” by keeping track of each item’s opened date, check estimate, notes, and finished status.

1. Adding an Item

You can add a new item using the add button in the app.

The main information you can register includes:

  • Photo
  • Item name
  • Category
  • Opened date
  • Check estimate
  • Notes

You can take a photo with your camera or choose one from your photo library.

2. Recording the Opened Date

Once you add an item, you can see how many days have passed since it was opened.

You can use Opelog for items whose opened dates are easy to forget, such as lotions, serums, toothbrushes, filters, seasonings, pet supplies, and household products.

3. About Check Estimates

By setting a check estimate, it becomes easier to see how many days are left until you should check an item, or whether the check date has already passed.

Check estimates do not guarantee a product’s expiration date or safety.

Please check the product label or the manufacturer’s guidance to determine whether an item is still safe to use.

4. About Notifications

If you allow notifications, you can receive reminders based on your check estimates.

If you do not receive notifications, please check the following:

  • Whether notifications for Opelog are allowed in your device settings
  • Whether notifications are set inside the app
  • Whether Focus mode or Do Not Disturb is enabled
  • Whether there are any issues with your device power or connection status

5. Finished Log

Items you have finished using can be saved to your Finished Log.

In the Finished Log, you can review the opened date, finished date, number of days used, and more.

This can help you decide when to buy a replacement or whether you want to purchase the item again.

6. About Premium

Opelog may offer Premium features.

Premium may include features such as:

  • Expanded item limit
  • Ad removal
  • Expanded archive features
  • Theme changes
  • Multiple photos per item

Premium features may change in the future.

Please check the latest details inside the app.

7. Data Storage

Opelog records are generally stored on your device.

If you delete the app, your saved data may also be deleted.

If you have important records, please check your device or OS backup settings.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Are my photos sent outside the app?
No. We do not send or store photos registered by users on our own servers.

Q. Is the check estimate the same as an expiration date?
No. The check estimate is only a reminder for checking the item. It does not guarantee the product’s expiration date or safety.

Q. Will my data be deleted if I delete the app?
Data stored on your device may be deleted when the app is deleted. Some data may remain in OS or external service backups.

Q. I purchased Premium, but it is not reflected in the app.
Please restart the app and try restoring your purchase. If it still does not appear, please check the Apple ID used for the purchase and contact us.

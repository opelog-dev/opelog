Opelog Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Opelog (the “App”) is an app for recording opened dates and usage status for cosmetics, skincare products, household items, and other items.

This Privacy Policy explains how user information is handled in the App.

1. Information We Handle

The App may handle the following information entered and saved by users within the App:

  • Item name
  • Category
  • Opened date
  • Check estimate period
  • Notes
  • Photos
  • Finished date
  • Repurchase status and favorite information

This information is entered by users in order to use the App’s features.

2. Data Storage

Item information, photos, notes, and other records created in the App are generally stored on the user’s device.

We do not collect or manage the content users record in the App on our own servers.

However, depending on device settings, OS backup features, or external services used by the user, data may be backed up outside the App.

3. Access to Photos

The App may request access to the camera or photo library in order to register item photos.

Selected photos are used for the item records chosen by the user.

The App does not send users’ photos outside the App without permission.

4. Notifications

The App may use notifications to remind users about check estimate dates and related items.

Notification permission can be enabled or disabled by the user at any time.

Even if notifications are not allowed, the App’s basic features can still be used.

5. Advertising

The App may use third-party advertising services to display ads.

Advertising services may use device information, advertising identifiers, and usage information for purposes such as displaying ads, measuring ad performance, and preventing fraud or abuse.

We do not sell users’ personal information.

6. In-App Purchases

The App may use in-app purchases to provide Premium features.

Purchase processing, payment information, refunds, and related matters are handled through systems provided by Apple Inc.

We do not directly obtain payment information such as credit card numbers.

7. Analytics

The App may use analytics tools to improve the App, understand issues, and review usage status.

Information used for analytics is handled in a way that does not directly identify individual users.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not primarily intended for children.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children without parental consent.

9. Data Deletion

Records saved in the App can be deleted by users within the App.

If the App is deleted, data stored on the device for the App may also be deleted.

For data stored in OS or external service backups, please check the settings of each service.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary.

If there are important changes, we will notify users within the App or on the official page.

11. Contact
